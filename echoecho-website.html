How to Play

Game Controls

  • Use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate the sound environment
  • Press Space to send out an echo
  • Use headphones for the best immersive experience

Objective

Navigate through a world of sound by sending out echoes and listening to how they return. Find hidden objects, avoid obstacles, and complete sound-based puzzles to progress through increasingly challenging levels.

Your success depends on developing your audio perception skills - the better you listen, the further you'll go!

Game Features

Immersive Audio

Experience 3D spatial audio technology that creates a truly immersive soundscape around you.

Multiplayer Mode

Team up with friends to solve complex audio puzzles that require cooperation and communication.

Adaptive Difficulty

The game adjusts to your skill level, ensuring both beginners and audio experts enjoy the challenge.

About EchoEcho

EchoEcho is a groundbreaking online game that challenges players to navigate and interact with a world through sound alone. Inspired by echolocation used by bats and dolphins, this game creates a unique sensory experience that develops your ability to perceive and interpret audio cues.

Each level presents new sound-based challenges, from simple navigation around obstacles to complex puzzles that require careful listening and quick reactions. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities that enhance your echoes, allowing you to perceive the virtual world in increasingly sophisticated ways.

Whether you're a casual gamer looking for something different, an audiophile fascinated by sound design, or an educator interested in developing auditory processing skills, EchoEcho offers a captivating and accessible experience for players of all backgrounds.

Put on your headphones, close your eyes, and discover a world where sound is your most powerful tool!
